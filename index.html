   Born in Mamaroneck, NY, Dylan Negri has a BFA in Interdisciplinary Sculpture from the Maryland Institute College of Art and also has a Concentration in ceramics. As a child growing up Dylan was drawn to many forms of expression. He played ice hockey for 12 years traveling all of the tristate area in New York and surrounding states. However, it wasn't before long that he found his Niche in the Arts. Starting Glass blowing and Ceramics at the age of 13, he soon relize that being a creator wa his true passion.
    After finishing his degree, he spent a little time in Baltimore and soon decided to move to Brooklyn, NY in 2014. It was there he found work as a contractor working for a wood/metal shop learning many trade skill and working on high end commercial and residential jobs throughout the 5 buroughs. During this time, he was able to continue his sculptural practice for 2 years and even showing some work in some high profile exhibisions and a museum. Dylan is now enrolled at the Parsons School of Design at the New School working for his MFA in Design and Technology.
